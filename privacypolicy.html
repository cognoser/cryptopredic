Privacy Policy

At TopCryptosNews, accessible from topcryptosnews.com, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by TopCryptosNews and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our service to you:

  • Log Files: TopCryptosNews follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.
  • Cookies and Web Beacons: Like any other website, TopCryptosNews uses "cookies". These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser type and/or other information.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected data for various purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our website.
  • To improve, personalize, and expand our website's features and performance.
  • To monitor website usage and diagnose technical problems.
  • To display personalized ads to support the running of this free site.

3. Google DoubleClick DART Cookie & AdSense

Google is one of the third-party vendors on our site. It also uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to topcryptosnews.com and other sites on the internet. However, visitors may choose to decline the use of DART cookies by visiting the Google ad and content network Privacy Policy at the following URL – https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

Google AdSense may automatically display advertisements on our website. You can learn more about how Google manages data in its ad products at https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

4. Your Privacy Rights

You may choose to block or disable cookies through your individual browser options. Detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers' respective websites.

5. Contact Us

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us through our Contact Page or via email at cognosser@gmail.com.
